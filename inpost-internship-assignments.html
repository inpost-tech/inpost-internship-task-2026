Welcome to InPost's internship recruitment. We've prepared two assignment tracks — one focused on software development, the other on quality assurance.

You don't have to pick just one. You can choose the track that best matches your interests, or complete both if you want to showcase a wider skill set. If you choose to do both, each assignment will be evaluated independently.

General information

Submission

Your work should be published in a public GitHub repository

When you're done, send us the link using the submission form.

AI tools

Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Cursor, etc.) is fully allowed and encouraged. You're responsible for every line you submit — be ready to explain your reasoning in a follow-up conversation.

Evaluation

We review every submission. We're not just looking at technical skills — we care about how you think, how you communicate, and how you approach problems that aren't perfectly defined. Candidates with the strongest submissions will be invited to the next stage.

Questions?

If something is unclear, make a reasonable assumption, describe it in your README.md, and move forward. Figuring out ambiguity is part of the task.

Good luck — we're looking forward to seeing what you come up with.
